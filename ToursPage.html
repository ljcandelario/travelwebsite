Featured Accommodation

Ica Valley Glamping, Wellington

December 2022

The Ica Valley Glamping destination is one of the premier glamping experiences near Wellington. Located on a private working farm between Wellington and Castlepoint, this private and peaceful accommodation is the perfect place to retreat, relax and go offline for a few days. The property is warm and cozy, has a separate outdoor kitchen, indoor toilet and shower, and an outdoor bathtub with a stunning view. Get ready to leave your problems behind in the city and be truly present in nature.
