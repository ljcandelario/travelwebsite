Adventure Awaits

"Travel is about finding the things you never knew you were looking for."

Destinations

West Coast

A 1,600km roadtrip through ancient rain forests and along the rugged coastline of New Zealand's wild west coast.

Queenstown

Located on the shores around Lake Wakatipu is the picturesque tourist town of Queenstown; also known as New Zealand's adventure capital.

Tongariro

Let's take a single day, 19km journey through one of NZ's Great Walks. An epic journey through an active crater, emerald lakes and volcanic landscapes.

Castlepoint

Let the open road take you on a memorable adventure to a small seaside settlement on the Wairarapa coast, with the Castlepoint Lighthouse as your stunning destination.

Blenheim

Come with us on a 21km half marathon as we explore the vineyards of the Marlborough region. No better way to end a beatiful long run than with a bottle of wine.

Cape Palliser

The southernmost point of NZ's north island is where you will find Cape Palliser, known for a small fishing village, a seal colony and a 250 step climb to a magnificent lighthouse.

Featured Article

Aoraki/Mt. Cook National Park

Aoraki/ Mt. Cook National Park, located in the south island of New Zealand, is one of the most popular outdoor tourist destinations in New Zealand. A magnificent national park that showcases some of the best walks in New Zealand. These beautiful walks range from short and beginner level to multi-day advanced walks. Come and visit Aoraki/ Mt. Cook National Park for the adventure of a lifetime. Aoraki/ Mt. Cook National Park is a 1-hour drive from Tekapo and is a premier destination for outdoor enthusiasts and mountaineers.
